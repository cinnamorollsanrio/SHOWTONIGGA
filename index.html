Sypnosis

Yuta Okkotsu once shared a promise with his childhood friend Rika, a bond so deep it felt unbreakable until a tragic accident changes everything. Rika returns, not as the friend he knew, but as a terrifying, uncontrollable curse that follows him everywhere. Consumed by fear and guilt, Yuta struggles to harness a power that could destroy anyone around him. Just when his life seems hopeless, the enigmatic sorcerer Satoru Gojo enrolls him at Tokyo Jujutsu High to learn control and purpose. There, Yuta meets other students with strange abilities and hidden strengths, including Maki, Toge, and Panda. Each challenges him to grow, teaching that courage and trust are as vital as raw power. But shadows of the past linger, whispering dangers that are both personal and deadly. Every step brings him closer to the truth about the curse, his own heart, and the mysterious bond with Rika.

Darkness does not wait. Suguru Geto, a sorcerer turned vengeful curse user, returns with a plan that threatens the world itself. Yuta must face enemies far beyond his imagination, testing both his newfound powers and his resolve. Friendships are tested and alliances form under the pressure of relentless battles. The streets of Tokyo become a battleground where survival is uncertain and the stakes are higher than ever. Each encounter forces Yuta to question what it truly means to protect and to forgive. With Rika’s spirit ever-present, he must navigate a maze of power, loss, and self-discovery. Every choice carries immense consequences that could tip the balance between hope and despair. Yuta’s journey is only beginning, and the secrets within his own heart may hold the key to everything.

Highlights

Stunning Animation & Fight Sequences

MAPPA delivers breathtaking animation with fluid, high-energy combat sequences that bring the world of jujutsu sorcery to life, capturing both epic battles and subtle character movements in stunning detail.

Heartfelt Storytelling

The film follows Yuta Okkotsu, haunted by the cursed spirit of his childhood friend Rika, as he struggles with grief and guilt. Guided by Satoru Gojo, he learns to control cursed energy and fights to break Rika’s curse while discovering his own confidence and purpose.

Characters & Voice Acting

The story features a cast of compelling characters, including Yuta, the cursed Rika, Maki Zen’in, Toge Inumaki, and Panda. Their distinct personalities and abilities are brought vividly to life by talented voice actors such as Megumi Ogata (Yuta) and Kana Hanazawa (Rika), adding depth and emotion to every scene.

Music & Cinematograph

The soundtrack, composed by Hiroaki Tsutsumi, Yoshimasa Terui, and Alisa Okehazama, perfectly complements the film’s visual storytelling, enhancing both emotional moments and intense action, while cinematography and editing provide a cinematic immersion that elevates the narrative.

Box Office Success

Jujutsu Kaisen 0” achieved massive commercial success, grossing approximately ¥13.8 billion in Japan and earning acclaim worldwide, cementing its status as a cultural phenomenon in the anime film industry.

Grief, Redemption & Self‑Discovery

Yuta’s journey is deeply shaped by his grief over Rika’s death, and through learning to accept his pain, he finds redemption and a renewed purpose — transforming his overwhelming curse into a source of strength and personal growth.

Yuta Okkotsu

Yuta is a soft-spoken and troubled teenager haunted by the powerful cursed spirit of his childhood friend, Rika. Unable to control her overwhelming power and fearing he might hurt others, he isolates himself until Satoru Gojo brings him to Tokyo Jujutsu High. There, Yuta learns to understand his emotions, accept his past, and grow into a capable sorcerer who fights to protect others.
